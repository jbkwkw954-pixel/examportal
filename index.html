Examination Rules & Terms of Use

Please read carefully before proceeding.

Mandatory Full-Screen Mode

Candidates must remain in full-screen mode throughout the examination. Exiting full-screen mode may result in the immediate termination of the assessment.

AI-Based Face Monitoring

The examination is monitored using an AI-powered facial recognition system that continuously tracks facial landmarks to verify the candidate's presence and identity.

Facial Calibration Requirement

Before the examination begins, every candidate must successfully complete the facial calibration process. The examination cannot proceed until calibration is completed.

Continuous Face Visibility

The candidate's face must remain clearly visible to the camera at all times. Prolonged absence from the camera frame may be treated as a security violation.

Browser and Window Restrictions

Switching browser tabs, minimizing the browser, or moving the examination window to the background may be detected as a violation of examination policy.

Violation Policy

A maximum of five (5) security violations are permitted. Exceeding this limit will automatically terminate the examination and invalidate the session.

Automatic Session Termination

The examination may be terminated automatically if any critical security breach is detected, including repeated violations or unauthorized activity.

Time Limit

The examination is time-bound. Candidates must complete all questions within the allocated time. Once the timer expires, the examination will be submitted automatically.

Academic Integrity

Candidates are expected to complete the examination independently without unauthorized assistance, reference materials, or external devices unless expressly permitted.

One Retake Rule

If a candidate does not pass, one retake attempt is allowed for the same test. After that retake, no further attempts will be permitted for the same course and week.

Data & Notifications

Your name, course, email address, exam result, and feedback responses will be sent to the Examination Administration for record-keeping and follow-up.

Final Decision

All examination activities are recorded by the system. The examination authority reserves the right to review security logs and make the final decision regarding the validity of any assessment.

Your data will be sent to Administration. This includes your name, course, email, exam result, and feedback answers.
